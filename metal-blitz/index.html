Metal Blitz

Side-scrolling run-and-gun inspired by classic arcade chaos. Placeholder art, but full combat loop: movement, dash, jump, rifle/rocket swap, grenades, enemy waves, miniboss.

  • Move: A / D or ← / →
  • Jump: W / ↑ / Space
  • Dash: Shift
  • Shoot: J (auto fire)
  • Rocket: K
  • Grenade: L

Everything is code-driven so you can drop in real sprites/tiles later.
